You never touch mining hardware. You never drain your battery.

When your feed needs sorting, when your upvote needs weight, or when your AI agent needs verifiable data — the open market handles the heavy computation on your behalf. You simply spend the energy-backed proof that dedicated Hashrate Providers print for you.

## Outsourcing the Print

When you perform an action that demands a Sharenote Bill (e.g., asserting consensus weight on a feed), the interaction happens seamlessly in the background:

1. **The Request**: Your wallet or client app recognizes that an action requires a proof-of-work bill (e.g., `20Z00`).
2. **The Outsource**: Your client delegates this requirement to a **Hashrate Provider** (a dedicated miner) over the network.
3. **The Fulfillment**: The Provider injects your specific job into their hardware, calculates the required energy, and returns the mathematically verifiable proof to your client.
4. **The Spend**: Your client attaches the mathematically verifiable PoW to your action, permanently asserting its consensus weight.

## What This Means for You

The computationally heavy process of establishing trust remains completely invisible to you.

- You can meaningfully sort feeds and data without manual verification.
- You pay for exactly what you request, on a per-action basis.
- Your personal device's battery and processing limit are never impacted.
